# Platform Prompting

Use these patterns to adapt prompts by platform ecology.

## 起点 / 纵横 / 17K

### Reader expectation

- clear power logic
- scalable world or conflict system
- visible protagonist agency
- chapter-end propulsion

### Prompt emphasis

- protagonist competence or growth vector should show early
- world detail should appear through action cost, not encyclopedia explanation
- each scene should either unlock advantage or deepen danger

### Prompt snippet

```text
Write in a commercially mature male-channel serial style for 起点-like readers.
Focus on capability, consequence, and forward propulsion.
Avoid lyric essay tone, broad philosophical reflection, and decorative metaphors that do not affect action.
```

## 番茄 / 七猫 / 书旗

### Reader expectation

- fast readability
- early hook confirmation
- clean emotional logic
- dense cliff momentum
- plainspoken, low-friction language
- scene-forward pacing over decorative prose

### Prompt emphasis

- open inside conflict
- explain only the minimum needed to cash the hook quickly
- shorten transition paragraphs
- keep chapter promises obvious
- prefer short-to-medium sentences
- let dialogue land quickly and usually stand as its own paragraph
- keep humor, reversals, and banter natural when the genre allows
- remove atmosphere-only description that delays payoff

### Prompt snippet

```text
Write for a mobile free-reading audience.
The first screen must deliver conflict or reversal immediately.
Prefer short-to-medium sentences, direct scene goals, and quick payoff loops.
Avoid slow atmospheric opening, repeated abstract emotion labels, academic neatness, and decorative metaphor chains.
Use colloquial written Chinese rather than bookish phrasing.
If slang or profanity appears, it must match the character and pressure of the scene instead of feeling forced.
Do not drift into cyberpunk-flavored jargon unless the chosen premise is actually cyberpunk or hard sci-fi.
```

## 晋江 / 红袖 / 潇湘

### Reader expectation

- intimate emotional perspective
- relationship tension
- memorable voice
- social nuance and subtext

### Prompt emphasis

- viewpoint should feel close to one body and mind
- attraction, resentment, embarrassment, and defense should show indirectly through action and speech
- let characters conceal things badly instead of saying everything cleanly

### Prompt snippet

```text
Write for a female-channel audience that values chemistry and emotional precision.
Do not summarize feelings before dramatizing them.
Let subtext leak through gesture, interruption, avoidance, and selective honesty.
Avoid universalized life lessons and neat therapeutic language.
```

## 飞卢

### Reader expectation

- sharp premise packaging
- immediate novelty statement
- accelerated escalation

### Prompt emphasis

- name the premise pressure early
- compress setup aggressively
- use strong event beats

### Prompt snippet

```text
Write with very high hook density and immediate premise exposure.
Do not spend more than a few lines before the core novelty becomes legible.
Avoid bloated explanation and ornamental introspection.
```

## Cross-Platform Rule

No matter the platform:

- do not ask the model to be "beautiful"
- do not ask for "rich diction" unless voice requires it
- do not ask for "high-quality prose" without structural constraints
- do not ask for "像底层人说话" as a blanket style target; ask for class-true, job-true, region-true speech only when the character setup supports it
- do not use profanity, memes, or hot slang as a universal anti-AI trick
- do not reduce AI flavor by making the prose intentionally crude or semiliterate

Those vague instructions often increase AI flavor instead of reducing it.
