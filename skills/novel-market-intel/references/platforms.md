# Platform Coverage

Use this file to decide which platforms matter, what reader ecology they suggest, and where to look first.

## Priority Platforms

### 起点中文网

- Best for: male-channel serialized fiction, especially fantasy, xianxia, urban, history, sci-fi
- Signal type: strong paid-reading ecosystem, durable long-form genre signals
- Look for: ranking charts, category charts, new-book charts, reader recommendation areas, official announcements
- Watch for: long-arc setup, clear power progression, commercial pacing

### 晋江文学城

- Best for: female-channel fiction, romance, fandom, youth, workplace, BL
- Signal type: intense tag ecology and reader preference clustering
- Look for: channel/category lists, quarterly or monthly exposure, user tag density, collection and comment heat where visible
- Watch for: emotional promise, CP chemistry, voice consistency, social-topic overlays

### 番茄小说

- Best for: fast-penetration mass-market fiction, mobile reading, free-reading ecosystem
- Signal type: broad traffic taste and hook efficiency
- Look for: discovery pages, category charts, recommendation channels, creator activity pages
- Watch for: strong first-screen hooks, low-friction readability, compact scenes

### 七猫小说

- Best for: free-reading commercial fiction, strong hook-led genre packaging
- Signal type: monetization-friendly mass appeal
- Look for: charts, category shelves, homepage promotion, creator/contract pages
- Watch for: title immediacy, familiar but efficient trope combinations

### 纵横中文网

- Best for: traditional web fiction with strong male-channel overlap
- Signal type: stable genre demand and contract-friendly categories
- Look for: rankings, category pages, editor recommendations, creator notices
- Watch for: broad-market hooks with slightly more conventional packaging

## Secondary Platforms

### 17K小说网

- Useful for: mainstream serialized genre taste and mid-platform comparison

### 飞卢小说网

- Useful for: hyper-fast pacing, high-density hooks, fandom, alternate-history, speculative mashups
- Warning: many top patterns are platform-specific and can overfit if copied literally

### 书旗小说 / 掌阅 / 红袖添香 / 潇湘书院 / 盐言故事

- Useful for: mobile-reading packaging, women-oriented segments, short-form or premise-first story products

## Source Priority

Use sources in this order:

1. Official platform ranking or category pages
2. Official creator notices and editorial recommendation areas
3. Official app/web discovery shelves
4. Credible recent summaries only when official signals are incomplete

## Signal Quality Heuristic

- High quality: repeated appearances across official charts and category shelves
- Medium quality: a single visible chart plus matching blurbs/tags across several books
- Low quality: one-off mention in a third-party article without corroboration

## Suggested Daily / Weekly / Monthly Reading Strategy

- Daily:
  - capture top visible books and recurring title patterns
  - focus on openings, blurbs, tags, and packaging language
- Weekly:
  - compare whether the same tropes hold across multiple refreshes
  - watch category stability and editorial reinforcement
- Monthly:
  - summarize what sustained, what faded, and what moved from niche to mainstream

## Platform Caveat

Do not assume one platform's hot format ports cleanly to another.
Always separate:

- `platform-native heat`
- `portable commercial pattern`
- `short-term anomaly`
